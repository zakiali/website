Zaki Ali

I'm a 4th year graduate student in Astrophysics at UC Berkeley. My research focuses on 21 cm cosmology and radio instrumentation. I work with Professor Aaron Parsons on two projects, the Precision Array for Probing the Epoch of Reionization (PAPER) and the Hydrogen Epoch of Reionization Array. (HERA), which both aim to detect a period in the universe when the first stars and galaxies formed. This transition, known as the Epoch of Reionization, is one of the last unexplored era's in the universe that has yet been detected.
